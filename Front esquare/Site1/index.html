Leading in the New Reality

The people with hearing and speech impairment are struggling to express their feelings in the society. They communicate with the help of sign languages. Sign languages use the visual manual modality to convey meaning. These languages have their own grammar and lexicon. The people who know sign language will be able to understand them. To overcome this struggle, we have created an app which focuses on hearing and speech impaired people to convey their emotions in their language. Our app provides the facility to record videos and that videos will be automatically generated with captions representing the meaning of sign language. The people unfamiliar with sign language can also understand the emotions they are expressing towards them. This app provides an option of automated voice for delivering the sign language effectively. That video along with captions or voice as per their choice can be saved to camera roll and used for better communication.

STATISTICS

Globally, 1.5 billion people live with some degrees of hearing loss.
34 million children have disabling hearing loss and is estimated that up to five out of every 1000 babies are born with hearing loss or acquire it soon after birth. An estimation says that 1.1 billion people (Aged between 12 - 35 yeears) are at risk of developing hearing loss due to noise exposure in recreational settings such as concerts and sporting events and through the use of personal audio devices.
